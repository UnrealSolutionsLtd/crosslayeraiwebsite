# CrossLayerAI - Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

## One-Line Definition

> **F2P games with active Discord communities where players have shareable moments but no automated way to celebrate them.**

## Decision Makers to Target

| Role | Why |
|------|-----|
| **Head of Community** | Owns Discord, feels the pain daily |
| **Product Manager/Producer** | Owns retention metrics |
| **CEO/Founder** | Small studios - they do everything |
| **LiveOps Lead** | Owns re-engagement campaigns |

---

## Pain Points They'll Recognize

1. "Our Discord is dead between content drops"
2. "Players churn and we have no way to bring them back"
3. "Push notifications don't work anymore"
4. "We have great moments but nobody sees them"
5. "Our community team can't keep up with content"

---

## Value Prop by Persona

### To Community Manager:
> "Your players have amazing moments nobody sees. We capture them and post to Discord automatically - your community stays active without you doing more work."

### To Product Manager:
> "You're losing 70% of players in week 1. We remember what each player did and reach them on Discord with personal messages that actually bring them back."

### To CEO/Founder:
> "Your players have great moments nobody sees. We fix that - and turn those moments into re-engagement that works."

---

## Sample Target List Criteria

When building lead lists, filter for:

- **Platform:** Mobile, PC, or cross-platform
- **Model:** F2P, freemium, or live-service
- **Genre:** Battle royale, shooter, roguelite, social casino, MMO, web3
- **Discord:** Active server with 1k+ members
- **Stage:** Launched and live (not pre-launch)
- **Team size:** 10-200 employees ideal

---

## Anti-Personas (Don't Pursue)

- Single-player narrative games
- Premium indie games ($20+ price)
- Hyper-casual publishers (no community focus)
- Games without social features
- Studios with no Discord presence
- Pre-revenue/pre-launch games (can't measure impact)
