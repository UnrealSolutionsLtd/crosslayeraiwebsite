## AI NPCs: Amazing In-Game, Invisible Outside

Platforms like **Inworld AI** and **Convai** have done incredible work making NPCs feel alive. Characters with personalities, memory, and emotional responses create immersive in-game experiences.

But here's the limitation: **when the player closes the game, the AI disappears.**

That amazing NPC who remembered your character's backstory? Gone until you launch the game again. The AI has no way to reach out, no presence outside the game world, no ability to re-engage players who've drifted away.
